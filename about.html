About Hive Real Estate

Welcome to Hive Real Estate, your trusted partner in finding the perfect property across India. We specialize in a comprehensive range of real estate solutions including residential sales, rentals, plots, agricultural land, farmhouses, and commercial properties.

With our extensive presence across major cities including Delhi, Gurgaon, Noida, Mumbai, and regions like Uttarakhand, Gujarat, Goa, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh, and Rajasthan, we bring you the finest properties from India's leading developers.

Our portfolio includes prestigious projects from renowned developers such as M3M, DLF, Signature Global, Adani, Whiteland, BPTP, Mahindra, Omaxe, Godrej, Vatika, and Bistech Smart World, ensuring quality and trust in every transaction.

At Hive, we understand that real estate is not just about properties; it's about finding the perfect space for your dreams to flourish. Whether you're looking for a modern apartment in Delhi NCR or a peaceful farmhouse in Uttarakhand, our expert team is here to guide you.

Our commitment to excellence, transparent dealings, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred choice in the real estate sector. We pride ourselves on our deep market knowledge and our ability to match our clients with properties that perfectly suit their needs and aspirations.
